How It Works

👁
Quality Check

Rejects blurry, tiny, dark, or overexposed uploads before inference.

🪄
Enhancement

Denoises, equalizes contrast, sharpens, and normalizes brightness.

🍃
Leaf Detection

Removes background and crops to the most relevant plant region.

🧠
Pruned AI

Runs a sparse neural network and reports confidence-aware guidance.
